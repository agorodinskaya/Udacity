Laughing Kookaburra

Kookaburras are famous for eating even venomous snakes that are longer than their own bodies.

  • Distribution: Eastern Australia.
  • Habitat:Trees.
  • Diet: Insects, crustaceans, small snakes and mammals.
  • Breeding: Laughing Kookaburras pair for life.

The laughing kookaburra is a bird in the kingfisher subfamily Halcyoninae.
It is a large robust kingfisher with a whitish head and a dark eye-stripe.
The upperparts are mostly dark brown but there is a mottled light-blue patch on the wing coverts.
